ABOUT
Although it sounds like something that only happens in sci-fi films, implanted electronic devices in human body has already applied widely, especially in medical field. In the future, with the development of the related techniques, we could boldly assume that implanted electronic devices will have a greater influence on daily life. For example, someone has implanted a memory card and cables which are able to transfer electrical signals between the memory card and neurons in brain. This person could set a specific action such as blinking twice quickly or saying ‘take a photo’ in mind as pressing the shutter, then the electrical signals received by retina will be stored and named with specific words. Whenever remind the specific words, and the target photograph will come out, just as real as seeing this photograph through screen or photographic paper.
